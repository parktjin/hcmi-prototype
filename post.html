Reflection After Hosting House Church Seminar for Lay Leaders - 평신도 세미나 주최 후 소감문
Author: 김영수 Date: 2026-04-28
Hits: 195 Recommended: 5

We are located in a small city called San Luis Obispo in California... Our church has been practicing house church ministry for the past 12 years. Currently, we have 14 English-speaking house churches and 2 Korean-speaking house churches.

We were truly blessed to host our very first Lay Leaders Seminar from April 10–12. As a church, we have committed to becoming a resource church for house church ministry...

저희 교회는 캘리포니아의 San Luis Obispo라는 작은 도시에 위치해 있으며, 약 5만 명의 인구와 인근에 대학교가 있는 지역입니다... 이번 세미나는 북미 지역에서 그 사명을 감당하기 위한 중요한 첫걸음이었습니다.

God showed us a rainbow, too.
